Our Story

From enterprise ambition to production-ready execution.

Enterprium was founded in 2016 in the United States on a clear conviction: the gap between enterprise ambition and technology execution is too wide, and too costly, to ignore. Most businesses are not short of ideas. They are short of the engineering capability, AI expertise, and delivery infrastructure required to turn those ideas into production-grade systems that work reliably at scale.

Starting from a founding team of engineers, architects, and AI specialists with years of experience inside large enterprise technology programs, Enterprium built a delivery model designed for the modern era. Our approach combines the depth of a senior engineering firm with the agility, speed, and innovation mindset of a technology startup.

Today, Enterprium operates as an AI-powered virtual software house with a business presence in San Francisco, California, supported by 300+ global team members across engineering, AI, automation, cloud, analytics, design, project delivery, client success, and business growth. We deliver intelligent systems, web platforms, automation workflows, CRM solutions, dashboards, and custom enterprise software for clients across 12+ countries.

Our growth and client expansion strategy is led by a dedicated revenue, partnerships, sales, and client growth team working closely with our engineering and delivery leaders to understand client needs, shape practical digital solutions, and ensure every engagement creates measurable business value.

Our delivery is human-supervised and AI-accelerated. Every project is guided by senior engineers, architects, delivery leads, and domain specialists. Every output is reviewed, tested, and refined to meet enterprise standards. This allows Enterprium to help businesses move faster, reduce execution gaps, and build digital systems that are practical, scalable, and ready for real-world use.
